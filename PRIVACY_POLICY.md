# Privacy Policy

**Dreifus Notes**
Last updated: April 19, 2025

## Overview

Dreifus Notes is a local-only note-taking app. All data stays on your device. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect any data.** Specifically:

- No account registration or login
- No analytics or crash reporting
- No advertising identifiers
- No location data
- No usage tracking

## Data Stored on Your Device

All notes and settings are stored locally on your device only:

| Data | Storage | Access |
|------|---------|--------|
| Notes and note content | Local database (encrypted on Android via SQLCipher) | You only |
| Note photos | Referenced from your device storage | You only |
| PIN codes | Local encrypted storage | You only |
| App settings | Local preferences | You only |

This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to any cloud service by the app itself (though your device's own backup system may include app data depending on your device settings).

## Biometric Authentication

The app may use your device's biometric authentication (fingerprint, face ID) to confirm sensitive actions such as data reset. Biometric data is processed entirely by your device's operating system (Android BiometricPrompt / iOS LocalAuthentication). The app never has access to raw biometric data.

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

- **Camera / Photo Library** — only when you choose to attach a photo to a note. No photos are uploaded or shared.
- **Biometric** — only when you initiate a biometrically-guarded action.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published in this repository. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
